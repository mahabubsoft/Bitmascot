Who We Are

Bit Mascot is a leading digital agency based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. With some of the most creative, talented minds in the business, Bit Mascot provides innovative, custom digital strategy and software solutions to our clients around the globe.

Founded in 2006, Bit Mascot has been evaluating, practising and perfecting how businesses connect with clients and customers. From creating stunning brand identities for new and established businesses, providing on-going support for meeting and exceeding business goals, creating engaging websites to building state-of-the-art software solutions, we have dedicated specialists that focus on the unique needs of your business.

Not only do we provide innovative, tailor made solutions for your business, we are also here for you every step of the way, long after the initial launch. We extensively research and integrate new aspects into our existing products that help us stay ahead in the rapidly expanding world of IT industry.
